# Kokotoa POS - User Guide

Welcome to the **Kokotoa POS** user guide! This document will walk you through everything from creating your account to managing a full-scale retail operation.

---

## 1. Getting Started

### Registration & Login
1.  **Create Account:** Go to the `/register` page. Enter your Name, Email, Phone Number, and a secure Password.
2.  **Verify OTP:** For your security, a one-time password (OTP) will be sent to your phone/WhatsApp. Enter this code on the verification page to activate your account.
3.  **Login:** Use your registered Phone Number and Password to log in.

### Store Setup
On your first login, you will be prompted to create your store:
1.  Enter your **Store Name** and **Address**.
2.  Select your **Business Category**.
3.  Once created, you will be redirected to the **Dashboard**.

---

## 2. Managing Your Inventory

The Inventory module is the heart of your business tracking.

### Adding Products
1.  Navigate to the **Inventory** tab.
2.  Click **"Add Product"**.
3.  **Required Info:** Name, Category, Buying Price, and Selling Price.
4.  **Optional Info:** SKU (Barcode), Unit (pcs, kg, etc.), and a Product Image.
5.  **Alerts:** Set a **"Minimum Stock"** level. The app will warn you when stock falls below this number.

### Adjusting Stock
If you receive new stock or find damaged items:
1.  Click the **Refresh (Adjustment)** icon next to a product.
2.  Select **"Stock In"** (to add), **"Stock Out"** (to remove), or **"Set Exact"** (to correct the count).
3.  Enter the quantity and a reason for the change.

---

## 3. Making Sales (Point of Sale)

The POS interface is designed for speed and simplicity.

### The Sales Workflow
1.  Navigate to the **POS** tab.
2.  **Find Products:** Use the search bar or browse by categories.
3.  **Add to Cart:** Click a product to add it to the cart. You can increase/decrease quantities directly in the cart.
4.  **Customer Details (Optional):** You can add the customer's name and phone number for future reference.
5.  **Payment:** Select the payment method:
    *   **Cash (Taslimu)**
    *   **M-Pesa / Tigo Pesa**
    *   **Bank Transfer**
6.  **Complete Sale:** Click **"Complete Sale"**. A professional receipt will be generated.

### Receipts
- You can view and print receipts immediately after a sale.
- All historical receipts are saved in the **Sales History** section.

---

## 4. Reports & Insights

Understand how your business is performing in real-time.

### Dashboard
- **Total Sales:** See how much you've made today and this month.
- **Top Products:** Identify your best-selling items.
- **Low Stock Alerts:** See a list of items that need restocking immediately.

### Detailed Reports
1.  Navigate to the **Reports** tab.
2.  Filter by date range (Today, Last 7 Days, Custom).
3.  View graphical trends of your sales and profit margins.

---

## 5. Team Management

If you have employees, you can give them their own access.

1.  Go to **Users / Management**.
2.  Click **"Add User"**.
3.  Assign a **Role**:
    *   **Admin:** Full access to everything.
    *   **Staff:** Can make sales and view inventory but cannot delete data or change settings.

---

## 6. Settings

- **Language:** Switch between **Kiswahili** and **English** anytime from the sidebar or settings page.
- **Profile:** Update your personal information or store details.
