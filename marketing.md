## 3. Deep Analysis of Problems Solved

| The Problem | How Kokotoa Solves It | ROI for the User |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Manual Record Keeping** | Digitizes every sale and movement of stock instantly. | Saves 2+ hours of manual work every day. |
| **Stock Loss/Theft** | Real-time tracking shows exactly what was sold and what is left. | Reduces "missing" stock by up to 90%. |
| **Inaccurate Reporting** | Automated reports calculate profit and trends without errors. | Helps make data-driven buying decisions. |
| **Multiple Payment Confusion** | Integrates Cash, M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, and Bank payments in one screen. | Faster checkout and easier reconciliation. |

---

## 4. Target Audience

1.  **Retail Shop Owners (Duka la Rejareja):** Looking for inventory control and fast sales.
2.  **Wholesalers (Wauzaji wa Jumla):** Need to track large movements of stock.
3.  **Pharmacies (Duka la Dawa):** Require expiry tracking and professional receipts.
4.  **Hardware Stores:** Need to manage thousands of diverse items with ease.

---

## 5. Visual Design Suggestions for Poster

*   **Colors:** Use the Primary Brand Color (vibrant blue/purple from the UI) contrasted with clean white space.
*   **Imagery:** Use high-quality photos of local Tanzanian shop owners using a tablet or phone with the Kokotoa interface (Glassmorphism aesthetics).
*   **QR Code:** Include a QR code on the poster for direct registration or demo access.
